Welcome to Reutor's Atar Calculator page. To get started just click the Add Subject button below, and if you can remember what your grades are just start typing them in! Some things to note:

  • If you haven't received your grades yet for some of your assesment than don't worry, as long as you type in at least one grade for each subject we will predict your other results!
  • If you leave one (or two) of the internal assessments blank (IA1, IA2 or IA3), our algorithim will assume that this assessment was cut from your corriculum due to corona virus.
  • If you already have an account here at Reutor (and you are logged in), your progress will be saved every time you click the calculate button so that you dont have to re-type your grades each time (so make sure you click calculate before you leave the page!).
  • If you don't have an account, just click the button in the top right corner of the page to make one (it's free). You need an account to receive your ATAR prediction.
